About Us

Hello! We are fourth year Management Engineering students at the University of Waterloo! The goal of the project is to help instructors make content that meets accessibility best practices and guidelines by making the process of creating this type of content efficient. 

Our tool aims to prevent information overload by providing clear and concise workflows that can be quickly and easily integrated into the professor’s current content creation process. This empowers professors to create accessible content and have accessibility as a primary consideration instead of an afterthought.
