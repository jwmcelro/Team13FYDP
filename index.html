Student Quote

“Accessible content...is very important for persons living with a visual impairment. Whether it is inaccessible links, no captions in photos or accessible headings, this excludes us from accessing content and essentially discounting our right to information. An accessible [course content] is one that I will enjoy navigating through and feel that my time is not compromised.”

Bernard Akuoko-Dabankah in department of Masters of Social Work at University of Waterloo
